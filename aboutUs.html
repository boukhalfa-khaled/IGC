About Us

who we are ?

The International General Certification Center is an institution registered in England and Wales under the number 15377750 to provide a certificate of competence in various fields to skilled people. We believe there are many talented and skilled individuals who lack formal recognition and opportunities to showcase their skills. Our platform seeks to bridge this gap by offering a rigorous testing process that assesses individuals’ skills and knowledge in their respective fields. Once a person passes the test, they receive a certificate that recognizes their competence and opens up new opportunities for them. Our platform is based on the principles of fairness, transparency and inclusiveness. We believe that everyone should have an equal opportunity to prove their skills and gain recognition for their work. We have designed our testing process to be free of bias and discrimination, and ensure that every candidate is treated with respect and dignity.

our mission

The mission of the International General Certification Center is to provide a platform for skilled people to showcase their abilities and gain recognition for their work . We believe that everyone deserves a fair chance to prove their skills and earn official recognition, regardless of their background or circumstances. Our mission is to create a more inclusive and equitable society by promoting merit and rewarding excellence.

For visitors who subscribe to our mailing list for discount coupons or updates on the latest products, we keep their email addresses to communicate and send offers.

Our values

We are guided by a set of core values that shape everything we do. These values include: Equity: We believe that everyone should have an equal opportunity to showcase their skills and gain recognition. Transparency: We are committed to transparency in all aspects of our platform, from the testing process to the certification process. Inclusivity: We believe in creating an inclusive platform that is accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or circumstances. Integrity: We are committed to upholding the highest standards of integrity and ethical behavior in everything we do. Excellence: We strive to excel in all aspects of our platform, from the quality of our testing process to the level of our partnerships

Our services

The IGCCenter platform offers a range of services designed to help skilled individuals gain recognition for their work These services include: Individual Testing: Our testing process is rigorous and designed to assess a person’s skills and knowledge in their field of specialization. The test process includes both written and oral examinations, as well as a practical component. Job testing  : We facilitate the process of selecting the appropriate competencies for the positions offered by employers and institutions by providing rigorous tests showing the competence of the applicants for the job. Certification: Once a person passes the test, they receive a certificate that recognizes their competence in their field. The person who passes the job test will also receive a level assessment certificate for all the conditions required by the job. Continuous training: Through partnerships with training centers and institutions we provide training opportunities for the continuous development of our tested personnel.

Benefits of using IGCCenter

There are many benefits to using the agreement for both individuals and organizations. Here are some of the main benefits: Official recognition : The IGCCenter platform provides formal recognition of a person’s skills and competence in their field, which can greatly enhance their professional reputation and career prospects. Our accreditation process involves rigorous testing and evaluation to ensure that only the most qualified individuals receive our seal of approval.

Access to opportunities:

By demonstrating their expertise through our certification program, individuals have access to a wide range of career opportunities, including career promotions, consulting engagement, and independent work. Our platform also connects accredited professionals with potential employers and customers who are looking for qualified and skilled candidates by spreading their assessment and experience across the platform.
