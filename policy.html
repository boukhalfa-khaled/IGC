Pravicy Policy

The igccenter platform is dedicated to safeguarding the privacy of users and customers, in compliance with local and international laws. Our mission is to provide the safest learning experience, ensuring that users feel secure while visiting the site. This page is dedicated to answering all questions regarding privacy.

what data we collect about users ?

Visitors can browse the site without providing any data, but like other websites, we use third-party mechanisms to collect information about the most visited pages, products, and articles. Third-party statistical services help us understand site traffic, popular products, and visitor preferences. The site uses cookies to store information about users' settings and other data needed for interaction with the browser. This automated data helps us learn about users' interests and suggest relevant courses.

For visitors who subscribe to our mailing list for discount coupons or updates on the latest products, we keep their email addresses to communicate and send offers.

i want to know more about cookies?

Cookies are essential elements left on your device by websites, including ours. They help activate your shopping cart, track your preferences, suggest courses, and present offers. Cookies are small text files stored on your browser or smartphone. They are created when visiting a website using cookies to track movements within the site and remember login information, preferences, and customization functions.

what date is collected about users ?

For registered users on the igccenter platform, we collect a username, password, and email address. Additional information, such as IP address, referring link, browser type, ultimate card charging location, purchase history, and phone number, is collected to communicate effectively, build trust, and offer personalized services.

about protecting visitors and ‘ users’ data

The security and confidentiality of information are paramount. We implement technical, administrative, and physical security procedures to prevent unauthorized access and misuse. Users are encouraged to set strong passwords, and we encrypt passwords on our databases by default.

you have right to update date on our website

Users can update contact information provided during registration, modify private choices, and add new shipping information. Users can request access to personal data, correct errors, and opt-out of direct marketing by contacting us via email at contact@igccenter.com.

We may update the privacy policy to comply with the latest laws, and by using our services, you agree to the policy.
